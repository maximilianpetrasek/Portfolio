So, who is this Max Petrasek guy?

Max Petrasek is an American Technical Artist who works primarily with digital interfaces. His style is colorful and expressive, especially his use of repeated patterns and solid colors. His use of eye-catching designs and simplistic coloring make his work stand out for illustration, publication, and game development.

In addition to his artistic skill, Max also has significant experience with most modern programming languages, such as C#, JavaScript, and SQL. As a hobby, he loves to create video games using game development engines such as Unity and Unreal.
